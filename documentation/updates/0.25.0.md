# 0.25.0 - Finally with Matcha
## General
- Reduced country rank of Arcturian Dominion from empire to kingdom
- Added magic gates to the mana density map mode
- Improved tooltips for the mana density map mode
- Merged Arcane and Dwarven mines into the normal base game ones

## National Cast
- The Arcanist is now part of the new National Cast
- You can switch to another Arcanist using a newly introduced character interaction 

## Politics
- The Arcane Council law is now a variant of Technocracy
- The Magocracy law is now a variant of Monarchy
- The Devout in the Arcturian Dominion are now called Lightwardens

## Races
- Reworked vampire 3D characters
- Replaced 2D bear race with 3D horned ogres (Thank you to the Anbennar)
- Replaced inappropriate combat unit type images

## Vampires
- There is now another way to deal with vampirism by removing their leader and integrating them into society
- Reworked some Vampirism events
- Added more events that will happen while the Journal Entry is active

## Fixes
- Fixed new Arcane Domains not showing in the Magic Panel
